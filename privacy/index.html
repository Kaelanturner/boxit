📦 BoxIt Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The short version: your data never leaves your device.

What BoxIt stores

Your moves, rooms, boxes, item lists, and box photos are stored locally on your iPhone. BoxIt has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. Nothing you enter is transmitted to us or to anyone else.

On-device intelligence

When you scan a box's contents with the camera, the photo is analyzed by Apple Intelligence entirely on your device. Photos and item lists are never uploaded.

Permissions BoxIt asks for

  • Camera — to scan QR labels and photograph box contents.
  • Location — to show your position on the moving map. Never stored or shared.
  • Calendar — only to add your moving dates as an event, and to remove that same event if you delete the move.
  • Notifications — for the moving-day reminders you choose.

Every permission is optional; BoxIt works without them.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions and the lifetime unlock are processed by Apple. BoxIt never sees your payment details.

What you share is up to you

Exported labels, manifests, room signs, and backups leave your device only when you share them yourself through the iOS share sheet.
