My QR label won't scan

Labels print best at 100% scale ("Actual Size" in the print dialog). If a label is taped over a seam or scuffed, open BoxIt → Scan and hold the camera 6–10 inches away; the code has built-in damage tolerance.

Where is my data stored?

On your iPhone, and synced privately through your own iCloud. Nothing is ever sent to us — see the privacy policy. You can export a full backup anytime from Settings → Your Data.

Restoring a backup

Settings → Your Data → Import Backup, then pick the backup file. Moves already on the device are skipped, so importing twice is harmless, and printed labels keep working after a restore.

Restoring purchases

Settings → Restore Purchases signs your Premium unlock back in on a new device.
